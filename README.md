# Think Step by Step

An AI-powered tutor that helps kids learn by thinking — not by looking up answers.

**Live app:** https://hackathon-0322-git-vercel-deploy-zd2372-6057s-projects.vercel.app/

---

## What it does

Instead of giving the answer right away, Think Step by Step breaks any question into small guided steps. The child answers one step at a time. If they get it wrong, they get a hint — not the solution. Once all steps are done, the full reasoning and answer are revealed.

---

## How to use it

### Step 1 — Create an account

When you open the app, you'll see a login panel on the left.

1. Click **"Register"**
2. Enter a **username** (used to log in)
3. Enter a **display name** (shown on the leaderboard)
4. Select your **age group** — this adjusts how the AI explains things:
   - **5–7** — Very simple steps, playful language
   - **8–10** — Clear steps with some thinking gaps
   - **11–14** — Fewer steps, more independent reasoning
   - **15–18** — Strategic hints, minimal hand-holding
5. Click **Register**

Next time, just type your username and click **Log in**.

---

### Step 2 — Ask a question

In the main area, type any question you want to work through. Good examples:

- `If 12 candies are shared among 3 friends, how many does each get?`
- `Why does ice melt when you hold it?`
- `What is the main idea of a paragraph?`

Then click **"Break it down"**.

---

### Step 3 — Answer one step at a time

The AI will break your question into 2–6 steps. For each step:

1. Read the prompt carefully
2. Type your answer or thinking in the box
3. Click **Submit**

**If you're correct** — you move to the next step and earn coins.

**If you're wrong** — you get a hint to guide your thinking. Try again!

---

### Step 4 — See the full solution

Once all steps are complete, you'll see:
- The full reasoning path
- The final answer
- What you did well
- What to work on next time

---

### Step 5 — Check your progress

Click **"Dashboard"** (top right) to see:

| Section | What it shows |
|---|---|
| **Stats** | Your total coins, best streak, sessions completed, and accuracy |
| **Leaderboard** | Top learners ranked by coins |
| **Recent Sessions** | Your past questions and how you did |
| **Learning Insights** | AI analysis of your strengths and patterns (click "Generate") |

---

## Coins & streaks

- Earn coins for every correct step
- Bonus coins for completing a full session
- Higher age groups earn more coins per step
- Your streak tracks how many correct answers you've given in a row

---

## Good questions to try

| Subject | Example |
|---|---|
| Math | `What is 15% of 80?` |
| Word problem | `A train travels 60 km/h for 2.5 hours. How far does it go?` |
| Science | `Why do objects fall to the ground?` |
| Writing | `How do I write a strong topic sentence?` |
| Logic | `If all cats are animals and Whiskers is a cat, what can we say about Whiskers?` |

---

## Tips

- You don't need to write a perfect answer — the AI understands partial answers
- If you're stuck, re-read the hint before trying again
- Younger age groups get more steps and more help; older groups are challenged more
- Your progress is saved — you can come back anytime
